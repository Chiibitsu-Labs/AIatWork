# Task brief: MERG workshop materials QR slide

Add a QR slide to the MERG workshop slide deck so attendees can download materials at the start of the session.

## What to do

Add **one slide** near the beginning of the MERG workshop deck (after the intro/welcome slide, before diving into content).

The slide should:
1. Display a scannable QR code linking to the shared Google Drive folder below
2. Have a short instruction line so people know what to do

## Slide copy

**Headline:** Grab the workshop files

**Body / instruction line:**
> Scan to download today's materials → keep them open on your phone or laptop during the session.

**Footer / small text:**
> aiatwork.chiibitsu.com

---

## Design

Match the existing workshop slide deck style. If no style guide exists, use:

- Background: `#F7F3E9` (cream)
- Headline colour: `#4E2A84` (purple)
- Body text colour: `#2B2330` (ink)
- QR code colour: `#2B2330` on white — make it large enough to scan from the back of a room (minimum ~250px / 5cm square on screen)
- Keep the slide uncluttered — QR code + copy only, no extra decoration

---

## Notes

- QR code only needs to point to the Drive link — no tracking params needed
- Do not use a URL shortener that might expire
- If the slide deck is in Google Slides: insert → image → upload the QR code as a PNG, or use a QR code add-on
- If the slide deck is in Canva or Keynote: same approach — generate QR as PNG from qr-code-generator.com or equivalent, then place on slide
- This slide is separate from the end-of-session signup slide (`/merg-updates`) — that one stays at the end for follow-up + certificates
